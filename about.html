How do I know if I should use KamShip for hiring or for finding a job? What’s the difference between the services offered for employers and job seekers?

The Team

A good starting point is to ask yourself: What do I need help with right now—finding the right talent or becoming the right candidate?
If you’re an employer struggling with long hiring processes or mismatched candidates, KamShip’s AI-powered job-matching platform is designed to speed things up by recommending candidates who actually fit your requirements—not just on paper, but in skills and readiness.
On the flip side, if you’re a job seeker, especially in Pokhara, and you’re tired of sending out resumes without results, KamShip can help bridge that gap. Whether you need to boost your confidence with training modules or stand out with certifications, this platform is here to prep you and get you noticed.
Think of it this way:
Employers use KamShip to cut down hiring time and get better matches.
Job seekers use KamShip to build skills, get certified, and land opportunities faster.
It’s not just a job portal—it’s a smarter, more personalized experience built to revolutionize how hiring and getting hired works in Nepal.
