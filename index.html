Revolutionize Hiring with AI & Training

Services we provide

KamShip Pvt. Ltd. aims to revolutionize Nepal’s hiring process by transitioning from traditional recruitment to an AI-driven digital platform integrated with training and certification services. The MVP will test the core hypothesis: **An AI-powered job-matching platform combined with basic training modules can reduce hiring time for employers and improve employability for job seekers in Pokhara.** The MVP focuses on delivering essential features to validate market demand, customer satisfaction, and scalability while keeping development costs low.

For job seekers

KamShip Pvt. Ltd. is transforming the recruitment landscape in Nepal by introducing an AI-powered platform that connects employers with the right candidates faster and more efficiently. Our mission is to streamline the hiring process by moving away from outdated methods and embracing smart, data-driven solutions.
Our MVP (Minimum Viable Product) focuses on the Pokhara region, combining intelligent job-matching with basic training and certification modules. This approach is designed to reduce hiring time for employers and enhance job readiness for seekers, ensuring a win-win for both sides. By validating this model, we aim to scale an affordable and effective hiring ecosystem across Nepal.

As part of KamShip Pvt. Ltd.'s mission to modernize recruitment in Nepal, our platform includes targeted training modules designed to upskill job seekers and enhance their employability. These short, practical courses focus on essential soft skills, digital literacy, and job-specific competencies. Integrated directly into our AI-powered job-matching system, the training ensures that candidates are better prepared for opportunities, while employers benefit from a more qualified and job-ready talent pool — reducing hiring time and improving outcomes for both parties.

At KamShip Pvt. Ltd., we offer industry-relevant training and certification programs designed to enhance the skills of job seekers and improve hiring efficiency for employers. Integrated with our AI-powered job-matching platform, these modules equip candidates with essential competencies that align with market needs. Upon completion, participants receive verified certifications, increasing their visibility and credibility to potential employers. This initiative is a key part of our mission to revolutionize Nepal’s recruitment process—starting with Pokhara.
